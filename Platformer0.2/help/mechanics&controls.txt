Mechanics:
	challenge - to get to the end of the level need to be able to jump a certain height/length
	
	1) Chicken leg - +0.5 blocks to jump height/length
	2) Bone - +~2 to jump length (and speed)
	3) Cheese - +1 life
	4) Hoover - has to be killed by jumping on it, doesn't give you anything, kills on contact
	
Controlls:
	A,D - move
	W - jump
	ESC - pause menu

	R - restart level
	G - noGravity mode
